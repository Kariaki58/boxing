Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need boxing equipment to start?
No special equipment is required to begin. We can design effective boxing workouts using bodyweight exercises. As you progress, we may recommend basic equipment like gloves or resistance bands, but we'll work with what you have available.
How fast will I see results?
Most clients notice improved energy and endurance within 2-3 weeks. Visible body transformations typically begin appearing after 4-6 weeks of consistent training. Results vary based on your starting point, consistency, and adherence to the nutrition guidance.
Can complete beginners join the program?
Absolutely! Our programs are designed for all fitness levels. Beginners receive foundational training focusing on proper technique and gradual progression. We adjust the intensity based on your current fitness level and experience.
Do you provide meal plans?
Yes, we provide customized nutrition guidance tailored to your goals (weight loss, muscle gain, or performance). This includes meal timing recommendations, portion guidance, and food choices - all adapted to UAE food availability and cultural preferences.
What if I travel frequently for work?
Our online program is perfect for frequent travelers. We design hotel-friendly workouts that require minimal or no equipment. Your training plan adjusts to your location and available facilities, ensuring you stay consistent regardless of where you are.
